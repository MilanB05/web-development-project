Introduction to good health and wellbeing




  • Health and wellbeing is all about having a balanced lifestyle. It includes having a balance between mind and body.It includes eating healthy food, staying active,being in touch with your friends and family and having a sense of purpose in life.
  • Wellbeing is not only about phisical health but also includes a mental healthlike for example how you manage stress or how you keep relations with your friend going.But also in the moments of despair not to be afraid of asking someone for help.
  • Health and wellbeing is about making a strong connection between your body and your mind.Taking care of your health also includes listening to your body and doing what it tells you to do.Knowing the sign that your body tells you and doing them you can achive amazing results.One powerfult quote by Buddha "The mind is everythin.What you think you become".

Types of mental health and wellbeing





  • Here, we are going to talk about different types of Wellbeing. In terms of different type of good health and wellbeing can be broken down into Phisycal health,Mental health,Emotional wellbeing,Social wellbeing,Spiritual wellbeing,Enviromental wellbeing.In the next paragraphs I'll tell you importance of each
  • Physical health is about keeping your body strong and working well.It includes having balanced diet, staying active, getting enough of sleep and avoiding bad habits. When your body is healthy you have more energy and you can handle tasks easily. Mental health relates to how you think,learn and make decisions.It involves having a clear mind, being able to focus, and sorting out challanges as they come up.
  • Emotional wellbeing is about understunding and managing your feelings. It means being able to show your emotions in a healthy way,staying positive during stress and arguments. Social wellbeing it targets your relations and ability to bond with people.It includes having a supportive friends and family.
  • Spiritual wellbeing is about having a sense of purpose in life.This is not necesserly religious.Environmental wellbeing means living clean,safe spaces and support your health.This includes your home, school, and community. A positive environment helps reduce stress and supports healthy habits.
